## Quick Start Guide

<Steps>
### 1. Creating Your Account

1. Click the "Sign In" button on the top right
2. Choose to sign in with your Google account or create a new account
3. Complete your profile setup

### 2. Uploading Your First Track

Once you're logged in:
1. Navigate to your tracks dashboard
2. Click the "Upload" button
3. Select your audio file
4. Add track details

### 3. Managing Your Tracks

In your tracks dashboard, you can:
- Play/pause tracks
- View track statistics
- Delete tracks
- Send tracks to other users
</Steps>
