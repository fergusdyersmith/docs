# Welcome to Trax

Trax is your all-in-one platform for managing, sharing, and tracking your music. This guide will help you get started with the basic features and functionality.

## Key Features

- **Track Management**: Upload and organize your music tracks
- **Analytics**: Track plays, saves, and shares for your music
- **Sharing**: Send tracks to other users and share your music
- **Multiple Format Support**: Support for various audio formats including MP3, WAV, and AAC
- **Waveform Visualization**: Visual representation of your audio tracks

## Getting Started

### 1. Creating Your Account

To get started with Trax:

1. Click the "Sign In" button on the top right
2. Choose to sign in with your Google account or create a new account
3. Complete your profile setup

### 2. Uploading Your First Track

Once you're logged in:

1. Navigate to your tracks dashboard
2. Click the "Upload" button
3. Select your audio file
4. Add track details like:
   - Track name
   - Genre tags
   - BPM (Beats Per Minute)
   - Cover image (optional)

### 3. Managing Your Tracks

In your tracks dashboard, you can:

- Play/pause tracks
- View track statistics (plays, saves, sends)
- Delete tracks
- Send tracks to other users
- Monitor track performance

## Navigation

- **Dashboard**: Your main hub for track management
- **Inbox**: Where you receive tracks shared with you
- **Saves**: Collection of tracks you've saved
- **Profile**: Your public profile and settings
