# Inbox Guide

<Callout type="info">
  Your Inbox is where you'll find all tracks that have been shared with you by other users.
</Callout>

## Understanding Your Inbox

Your Inbox serves as a central hub for:
- Receiving tracks from other users
- Managing incoming collaborations
- Organizing shared content
- Responding to track shares

## Managing Received Tracks

<Steps>
### Viewing Received Tracks

When someone shares a track with you:
1. You'll receive a notification
2. The track appears in your Inbox
3. You can see:
   - Track title
   - Sender's name
   - Date received
   - Any attached message

### Track Actions

For each received track, you can:
- Play/preview the track
- Save to your library
- Reply to the sender
- Forward to others
- Delete from inbox
</Steps>

## Inbox Management

<Callout type="tip">
  Keep your inbox organized by regularly processing received tracks.
</Callout>

### Best Practices

- Review new tracks promptly
- Save important tracks to your library
- Archive processed items
- Use filters to find specific tracks
- Respond to senders when appropriate

## Collaboration Features

When receiving collaborative tracks:
- View collaboration permissions
- See edit history
- Add comments
- Suggest changes
- Track version updates

## Storage Management

<Callout type="warning">
  Inbox tracks count towards your storage quota until deleted or archived.
</Callout>

### Storage Tips

- Archive processed tracks
- Delete unnecessary items
- Save important tracks to library
- Monitor storage usage
